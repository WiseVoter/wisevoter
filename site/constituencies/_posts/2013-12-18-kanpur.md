## Constituency details
Kanpur Nagar Lok Sabha constituency (Hindi: कानपुर लोक सभा निर्वाचन क्षेत्र) is one of the 80 Lok Sabha (parliamentary) constituencies in Uttar Pradesh state in northern India.
