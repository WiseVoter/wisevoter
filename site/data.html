List of Data Sets on WiseVoter

In addition to the detailed data on politicians, parties, constituencies - WiseVoter hosts the following data sets.
  1. /data/mpslist.json: This dataset documents all the MPs in last 15 Lok Sabhas including their gender.
  2. /data/2009-general-elections.json: This dataset documents the results and voting engagement from 2009 general elections.
  3. /data/phases.json: This dataset documents the phases of 2014 Indian General elections and which constituencies go to election when.
Please report any anomalies or updates to these datasets in the comments below.
