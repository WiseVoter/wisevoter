List of Data Sets on WiseVoter

In addition to the detailed data on politicians, parties, constituencies - WiseVoter hosts the following data sets.
  • /data/mpslist.json This dataset documents all the MPs in last 15 Lok Sabhas including their gender.
  • /data/2009-general-elections.json This dataset documents the results and voting engagement from 2009 general elections.
  • /data/phases.json This dataset documents the phases of 2014 Indian General elections and which constituencies go to election when.
Please report any anamolies or updates to these datasets in the comments tab.
