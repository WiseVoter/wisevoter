Kaiserganj

Uttar Pradesh

Constituency details

Kaiserganj Lok Sabha constituency (Hindi: कैसरगंज लोक सभा निर्वाचन क्षेत्र) is one of the 80 Lok Sabha (parliamentary) constituencies in Uttar Pradesh state in northern India.
