This article visualizes voter participation in 2009 Lok Sabha elections. With a young population and a thriving democracy the 2014 Indian elections are feast to watch and follow. Remember to vote!

Using the data in references and WiseVoter's JSON API, http://www.wisevoter.com/data/2009-general-elections.json, I have created the following map which shows average turnout by the states in 2009 general elections which formed the 15th Lok Sabha. Hover on the state with your mouse or touch to get the info!
